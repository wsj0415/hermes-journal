## 20 个 SEO 提示词系统

### GBP 审计（8 个）

#### 1. GBP 类别审计

**为什么从这里开始**: 如果类别错误，其他都不重要。

**核心洞察**:
- GBP 有主类别和次类别
- 大多数业主创建列表时选主类别后从未触碰
- 竞争对手添加了不知道的次类别
- 这些类别直接控制哪些搜索触发地图包列表

**错误类别 = 高意图搜索不可见**。

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to Google Maps. Search '[service] in [city]' 
for these 3 keywords: [keyword1], [keyword2], [keyword3]. 

For each search, note which competitors show up in Map Pack. 
Then open each competitor's GBP listing and extract their 
primary category and all secondary categories. 

Put everything in a spreadsheet. One tab per keyword. Columns: 
business name, primary category, secondary categories, star 
rating, review count, ranking position. 

Highlight any categories competitors have that I'm missing. 
Then give prioritized list of categories to add, ranked by 
how many top competitors have them. Start with ones all 3 
competitors share — non-negotiable. End with ones only 1 
competitor has — differentiation opportunities.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 有客户添加一个次类别后下周开始为新搜索展示
- **本地 SEO 最快胜利**
- 模式识别：映射类别 against 地图包排名开始看到模式
- 如每个"emergency plumber"排名业务都有"water damage restoration"次类别

---

#### 2. GBP 属性审计

**核心洞察**:
- 属性是个人资料上的小标签
- "veteran-owned" / "free estimates" / "24/7 availability" / "accepts credit cards"
- Google 用这些匹配搜索者和企业
- 竞争对手有你没有的属性 — 保证

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to my GBP at [URL] and competitors: 
[URL1], [URL2], [URL3]. For each listing, extract every 
visible attribute and tag.

Put in spreadsheet. Columns: attribute name, my listing (yes/no), 
competitor 1/2/3 (yes/no). Highlight attributes competitors 
have that I'm missing.

Create three lists:
1. Attributes ALL top competitors have — table stakes, need immediately
2. Attributes 2/3 competitors have — strong recommendations
3. Attributes only 1 competitor has — differentiation opportunities

For each missing attribute, tell likely ranking impact 
(high/medium/low) and whether affects CTR.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 属性是双赢：帮助排名更具体搜索 AND 增加点击率
- "baseline requirements"是关键洞察
- 如果所有前三竞争对手有"free estimates"而你没有 — 不是可选，是入场券

---

#### 3. 竞争对手评论拆解

**核心洞察**:
- 星级几乎不说明什么
- **真正重要的是评论速度** — 他们获得新评论多快对比你
- 200 条评论 180 条两年前获得的企业弱于 90 条评论每月获得 15 条的企业
- Google 追踪这个

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to competitor GBP listings: [URL1], [URL2], 
[URL3]. For each competitor, read last 50 reviews. Extract:

- Total review count
- Average star rating
- Reviews in last 30/60/90 days
- Most mentioned services (top 5)
- Most mentioned neighborhoods/cities (top 5)
- Most mentioned staff names
- Recurring complaints/negative themes
- Top 5 keywords/phrases to train customers to mention

Compare review velocity to mine at [my GBP URL]. Output 
spreadsheet with all data + separate tab saying exactly how 
many reviews/month I need to catch top competitor and how long.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 第二标签是未来 6-12 个月完整评论策略
- 知道确切每月需要多少评论
- 更深层洞察：看客户评论中提及什么
- "great furnace install in highland park" 正为竞争对手做 SEO 工作
- 带关键词和地点名称的评论是排名信号

---

#### 4. 评论回复策略

**核心洞察**:
- Google 确认回复评论改善本地排名
- 大多数企业不回复或粘贴相同"thanks for your review!"
- **回复是关键词和服务提及的免费广告位**
- 如何处理负面评论直接影响新客户信任

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to my GBP at [URL] and competitors: 
[URL1], [URL2], [URL3]. Analyze last 30 review responses 
from business owner. Extract:

- Reviews with responses vs no response (calculate %)
- Average response time
- Whether mention specific services/locations
- Response length and tone
- How negative reviews handled (apologize/offer resolution/defensive?)
- Language patterns that perform well

Build review response template system:
- 3 variations for 5-star (include service + city keywords)
- 3 variations for 4-star (acknowledge gap, invite return)
- 3 variations for 3-star (accountability + resolution)
- 3 variations for 1-2 star (professional, empathetic, defuse)

Each template 40-80 words. Sound like real person, not robot.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 模板系统是真正价值 — 任何评论 60 秒内回复
- 10 条评论/月，每回复提及服务和城市 = **每年 120 条关键词丰富内容**
- 之前没有的

---

#### 5. GBP 帖子策略

**核心洞察**:
- GBP 帖子是平台最未充分利用功能
- 帖子直接显示在列表上，7 天后过期
- 一致发布向 Google 发信号企业活跃
- **活跃企业获得优先展示**
- 竞争对手可能没发布 — 这是优势

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to my GBP at [URL] and competitors: 
[URL1], [URL2], [URL3]. Check their GBP posts section. Record:

- Total posts in last 90 days
- Post frequency (posts/week average)
- Post types (offer/update/event/product)
- Whether include images and CTAs
- Topics/themes covered
- Seasonal/promotional patterns
- Estimated engagement

Build 8-week GBP posting calendar with 2-3 posts/week:
- Seasonal service promotions
- Before-and-after project showcases
- Neighborhood-specific posts ([area1], [area2], [area3])
- Review highlights
- Team spotlights
- Educational posts about common problems

Each post: 100-150 words, clear CTA, image description, 
naturally include target keywords. Write full copy for 
weeks 1-4, detailed outlines for weeks 5-8.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 竞争对手分析几乎总是显示同样事：没人发布
- 一致发布立即让你脱颖而出
- **社区特定帖子建立位置相关性极难复制**
- 每次发布提及"just completed kitchen remodel in [neighborhood]"建立位置相关性
- Google 看到企业与该区域关联
- 每月 8-10 个社区建立本地权威

---

#### 6. 服务板块优化

**核心洞察**:
- Google 给整个板块列服务带描述
- **这是黄金关键词地产，几乎没人优化**
- 大多数留空或添加无描述服务
- 像只有标题无内容的落地页

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to my GBP at [URL] and competitors: 
[URL1], [URL2], [URL3]. Extract complete services section:

- Every service listed
- Whether has description
- Description detail level
- How structured (problem-solution/feature-benefit/keyword-heavy)
- Services in every competitor's (non-negotiable)
- Services in some but not all (opportunities)

Audit my services vs website [URL]:
- Services on site but not in GBP
- Services in GBP with no description
- Services where description weaker than competitors

Write optimized descriptions for all services:
- 2-3 sentences, 40-60 words
- Naturally include primary keyword
- Mention specific service area
- Include benefit/outcome
- Sound like real business, not SEO tool
```

**为什么重要**:
- 服务板块是 GBP 上唯一完全控制文本的地方
- 评论由客户写，Q&A 可来自任何人
- **服务描述 = 你的文案，你的关键词，你的信息**
- 交叉引用网站捕获常见错误：添加新服务到网站但忘记更新 GBP
- 如果做"trenchless sewer repair"但不在 GBP 上 — 地图包中该搜索不可见

#### 7. GBP 描述优化

**核心洞察**:
- GBP 描述是 750 字符黄金地产
- 大多数浪费：留空/从网站复制粘贴/关键词堆砌听起来机器人

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to my GBP at [URL] and competitors: 
[URL1], [URL2], [URL3]. Extract each business's full GBP 
description. Spreadsheet columns:

- Business name
- Full description text
- Character count
- Primary keyword used (yes/no + which)
- Secondary keywords mentioned
- Service areas mentioned
- Trust signals mentioned
- USPs mentioned
- CTA included (yes/no + what)
- Overall tone (professional/casual/urgent/local)

Analyze what ALL top competitors mention (non-negotiable), 
what no one mentions (could own), what I'm saying nobody 
else says.

Write 3 versions (max 750 chars each):
- Version 1: Keyword-focused for max ranking signal
- Version 2: Conversion-focused for max calls
- Version 3: Trust-focused (experience/reviews/credibility)

All include keywords + service areas. Sound human, not marketing.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 3 个版本可测试
- 运行版本 1 三十天，检查印象和电话，然后试版本 2
- 大多数人写一个描述永远忘记
- **视为可测试资产给你复合优势** — 市场没人做

#### 8. GBP 照片审计

**核心洞察**:
- Google 说带照片企业获得**42% 更多方向请求和 35% 更多点击**
- 不仅是拥有照片 — 是对的照片一致上传
- 大多数 3 年前上传 10 张模糊手机照就完成
- 主导地图包的竞争对手每周上传，有建立信任的前后对比照

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to my GBP at [URL] and competitors: 
[URL1], [URL2], [URL3]. Analyze photo section completely:

- Total photo count
- Photos uploaded last 30/90 days
- Photo types (team/jobs/before-after/trucks/equipment/office)
- Photo quality (professional vs phone)
- Any stock photos
- Include people/faces
- Show specific neighborhoods/landmarks
- Average photos/week frequency

Build 8-week photo upload plan:
- Exact photos/week to beat top competitor by 50%
- Specific shot list each week (what/where/why)
- Which weeks prioritize before/afters vs team vs trucks
- Naming convention with service keywords + location names
- Geotagging instructions for neighborhoods

No generic office photos — every photo as ranking signal.
```

**为什么重要**:
- **一致性胜过数量**
- 一天上传 50 张然后 6 个月无 = 告诉 Google 不活跃
- 每周上传 3-5 张优质照片 = 告诉 Google 企业活跃繁荣
- 命名约定和地理标记说明本身就值得 — 大多数企业不知道照片元数据影响排名

### 网站审计（7 个）

#### 9. 关键词差距审计

**核心洞察**:
- 找到竞争对手排名为你没有的每个关键词
- **这是你网站应产生收入隐藏的地方**

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and log into SEMrush. Go to Keyword Gap tool. 
Enter my domain [domain.com] and 3 competitors: [c1], [c2], [c3].

Filter: Keywords where competitors rank positions 1-20 but 
I don't rank at all. Export.

Further filter:
- Monthly volume 100-2,000 (local intent sweet spot)
- Contains: [city], [service], "near me", "emergency", "best", "local"
- Keyword difficulty under 40

For top 20 keywords:
- Search volume, difficulty
- Which competitors rank + position
- Whether have existing page to optimize
- Whether need new page

Output spreadsheet sorted by opportunity score 
(high volume + low difficulty + competitors ranking = highest).
Add "Action Required" column: "Optimize existing" or "Create new".
```

**为什么重要**:
- 大多数企业对竞争对手每天赢的搜索不可见
- 此审计显示确切收入差距在哪和如何应对
- 过滤列表移除噪音只给值得追求的关键词
- 有真实买家意图和真实搜索量的

#### 10. 资金页面审计

**核心洞察**:
- 大多数本地企业网站有错误页面排名，或无页面排名
- **大多数坐在第 2 页排名，一次标题标签更改就到第 1 页**

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and log into Google Search Console for [property]. 
Go to Search Results, date range last 3 months, export all 
queries and pages.

For each page:
- Keywords ranks for
- Average position per keyword
- Impressions and clicks
- Whether optimized for keyword or ranking accidentally

Identify:
- Pages ranking positions 4-15 for high-value keywords 
  (need one optimization push to top 3)
- Pages with high impressions but low clicks 
  (title/meta description problem)
- Pages with zero rankings 
  (dead weight or untapped opportunity)
- Keywords ranking on wrong page 
  (cannibalization issue)

Build priority action list:
- Quick wins: Optimize this week
- Medium effort: Rebuild next month
- Long term: New pages next 90 days

Include: current position, target position, time to improve, 
exact on-page changes needed.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 此审计找到所有并告诉确切先修复什么
- 无猜测，无浪费精力

#### 11. 服务 + 城市页面构建器

**核心洞察**:
- 多城市排名最快方式：每服务每城市专用页面
- 大多数有一个通用服务页面，奇怪为什么 30 分钟外城市不展示

**提示词**:
```markdown
I need location-specific service pages. Primary service: 
[service], cities: [city1-5], website: [URL], target keywords: 
[service]+[city], [service] near [city], best [service] in [city].

Check website and tell which city+service pages exist/missing.

For each missing, write fully optimized page:
- SEO title <60 chars (service + city)
- Meta description <155 chars (service + city + reason to click)
- H1 heading (service + city natural)
- Opening paragraph 100 words (specific city pain point)
- Why choose us 150 words (local landmarks/neighborhoods/challenges)
- Service details 200 words (what included/process/customer gets)
- Social proof with city review placeholders
- FAQ with 3 city-specific questions
- CTA with phone + "Call now for same-day in [city]"

Include: exact URL slug, 3 internal linking opportunities, 
2 local citation directories.
```

**为什么重要**:
- **Google 排名页面非网站**
- 如果没有专门关于 [service] in [city] 的页面 — 不会为该搜索排名
- 此提示词一次会话构建整个位置页面栈
- 需要作家团队数周的工作午餐前完成

#### 12. Google Search Console 分析

**核心洞察**:
- 大多数企业主登录 GSC，不知所措，关闭标签
- **第 2 页关键词是所有 SEO 中最低垂果实**
- 有人已在搜索你提供的 — 只差一次优化推动就可见

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and log into GSC for [property]. Export last 
90 days: all queries, pages, clicks, impressions, CTR, position.

Find page 2 goldmine: Keywords ranking positions 11-20 with 
100+ impressions/month. These are highest priority — jump from 
15 to 5 worth more than creating 10 new pages.

For each page 2 keyword:
- Keyword in title tag?
- Keyword in H1?
- Keyword in first 100 words?
- Page word count?
- Internal links pointing to it?
- Current meta description?

Build 30-day optimization sprint:
- Week 1: Title tag + H1 fixes for top 10 page 2 keywords
- Week 2: Content additions for thin pages (<500 words)
- Week 3: Internal linking fixes (which pages link to which)
- Week 4: Meta description rewrites for high impressions/low CTR

Write exact new copy for every fix — actual title tag, H1, 
meta description to use.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 第 2 页关键词是最低垂果实
- 此提示词找到每个并告诉确切写什么

---

#### 13. 评论情感分析

**核心洞察**:
- 大多数机构不知道存在的提示词
- 取竞争对手评论反向工程客户使用的确切情感语言
- 然后构建语言到你的网站文案/GBP 内容/评论请求脚本
- **结果是在肠道层面共鸣的内容** — 用实际客户用词写

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and go to competitor GBP listings: [URL1], [URL2], 
[URL3]. Read last 100 reviews each. Deep sentiment analysis:

Extract:
- Top 20 emotional words customers use (relieved/impressed/finally/trustworthy)
- Top 10 specific outcomes (fixed in one visit/no mess/arrived on time)
- Top 5 fears/frustrations before service (cost a fortune/companies canceling)
- Exact phrases customers use when recommending (money phrases for website)
- Language patterns in 5-star but not 3-star reviews

Same analysis for my reviews at [my GBP URL]. Compare sentiment 
and language — where are emotional gaps?

Rewrite using this data:
- GBP description using emotional language real customers use
- Homepage headline and subheadline
- Review request script so customers naturally use right words
- 3 social proof statements mirroring how best customers talk
```

**为什么重要**:
- 大多数企业写关于自己的网站文案
- **主导企业用客户实际用词写文案**
- 评论情感分析找到那语言 — 让陌生人信任足够打电话的确切词
- 这就是本地企业转化率优化实际样子

### 外链审计（5 个）

#### 14. 竞争对手外链审计

**核心洞察**:
- 外链是信任转移
- 不需要数百，需要对的
- **每月 2-4 个真实上下文链接比 20 个随机目录提交复合更快**

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and log into Ahrefs. Go to Site Explorer, enter 
[competitor1]. Export full backlink profile:

Filter: Dofollow only, DR 20+, traffic 100+/month, not sitewide.

Repeat for [competitor2] and [competitor3].

Find link opportunities:
- Domains linking to ALL 3 but not me (highest priority)
- Domains linking to 2 but not me (medium priority)
- Domains linking to 1 but not me (worth reviewing)

For each high-priority:
- Domain name + URL
- DR
- Site type (directory/news/blog/association/sponsor)
- How competitor earned (guest post/sponsorship/citation/PR)
- Realistic chance (high/medium/low)
- Exact outreach strategy

Build 90-day link building plan:
- Month 1: 5 easiest (directories/citations/associations)
- Month 2: 5 medium (local news/sponsors/guest posts)
- Month 3: 5 authority (industry pubs/government/universities)

Include contact method + full outreach email for each.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 此审计移除所有猜测
- 确切知道 target 谁/他们如何获得链接/外展说什么

#### 15. 本地引用审计

**核心洞察**:
- 引用是 Google 验证企业真实的方式
- **跨目录不一致 NAP 数据是最常见本地 SEO 杀手之一**
- 大多数企业不知道多少错误引用正积极对抗排名

**提示词**:
```markdown
My business info: Name: [exact], Address: [exact], Phone: [phone], 
Website: [URL].

Search for business across: GBP, Yelp, Bing, Apple Maps, 
Facebook, BBB, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack, Houzz, Yellow 
Pages, Manta, Foursquare, Superpages, Citysearch, industry 
directories.

For each platform record:
- Listing exists?
- Business name (exact)
- Address (exact)
- Phone (exact)
- Website URL
- Duplicate listings?
- Star rating + review count

Spreadsheet with all findings, flag inconsistencies in red.

Priority fix list:
- Which inconsistencies most damaging
- Step-by-step correction instructions
- High-value directories with no listing (create immediately)
- Monthly citation maintenance checklist
```

**为什么重要**:
- Google 跨数百来源交叉引用企业信息
- 如果电话在 Yelp 和 Google 不同 — 产生信任信号冲突抑制排名
- **修复引用是少数 30 天内可见真实排名改善的 SEO 任务之一**

#### 16. 本地搜索意图映射

**核心洞察**:
- 大多数企业优化错误关键词
- 追逐高量感知词忽略低量高意图搜索实际产生电话
- 映射整个关键词宇宙到买家旅程阶段

**提示词**:
```markdown
Open Chrome and log into SEMrush. Pull all keywords in niche 
for service area with 20+ volume/month.

Categorize into 4 stages:

Stage 1: Problem-unaware
- Has problem but doesn't know what called
- "water coming through ceiling" / "AC making weird noise"

Stage 2: Problem-aware
- Knows problem but researching solutions
- "how to fix leaking roof" / "why AC not cooling"

Stage 3: Solution-aware
- Comparing options and providers
- "plumber vs DIY pipe repair" / "how to choose HVAC company"

Stage 4: [Continues...]

Prioritize by stage + intent.
```

**为什么重要**:
- 告诉确切优先哪些关键词
- 高意图搜索实际产生电话
